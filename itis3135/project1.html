Project Design

Overview

Description:-

This webpage is dedicated to showcasing the passion of my client, Jeet Patel, for the sport of cricket and its various aspects, including game strategies, player statistics, and latest news updates. Jeet Patel's enthusiasm for cricket is unparalleled, and this website aims to share that passion with the world. Basically an intro to what cricket is and how its played across the globe.

Intended Users:-

The target audience for this website will be cricket enthusiasts who are keen to gain knowledge about the sport. The site is likely to attract users who are seeking to expand their understanding of the game, as well as those who are searching for cost-effective alternatives.

Content Overview:-

In general, this website aims to educate its users about cricket sport and provide information about its affordable alternatives. Additionally, it will endorse cricket brands like Indian Premier League, Big Bash League, Caribbean Premier League, etc. The website's objective is to help users explore and cultivate their interest in cricket sport.
