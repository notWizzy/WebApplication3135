Website Evaluation


CRAP: Contrast Repetition Alignment Proximity


Worst Website

This website does not follow the principles of CRAP

  • C - Contrast

    • Reading the elements on the website is really difficult since it has too many colors and hard to focus on one single element.

  • R - Repetition

    • The left column of the website is full of stacked hyperlinks, so many small fonts repeating all over the webapge, which loses interest as a suer of the site.

  • A - Alignment

    • The images are in a format of collage, but not properly aligned making it look very odd and focus on one thing at a time.

  • P - Proximity

    • Everything on the page is forced together leaving no free space other than the sides.
